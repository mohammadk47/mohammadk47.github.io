Join Us

At Extendicare, we strive to provide a diverse work environment in which employees are respected as professionals and there is open communications between all disciplines. We hire people with the skill, knowledge and talent to develop and implement innovative programs and services to improve the living and working environment for residents and the team members who care for them. Employees are truly our greatest asset. If you join the Extendicare team, your continued growth and career development is critical to our success and we will provide you with the required opportunities to expand your abilities. If you are interested in Extendicare’s team approach, ask us for more information and join us.
