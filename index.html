Organizational Background

In 1968, Harold Livergant, a visionary in the field of health care, and John MacKay, his business partner, founded Extendicare. Mr. Livergant dedicated his life to raising the standard of care provided to senior citizens in Canada. The company's vision, mission, and values are based on his unwavering passion for quality. From the first house it bought in 1969 in Ottawa, Extendicare has expanded to rank among Canada's biggest providers of home care and long-term care services. It is now a well-respected industry leader in the provision of superior, cutting-edge, and patient-centered care. Skilled nursing care, home health care, retirement living, management, and consulting services are just a few of the ways that Extendicare's knowledgeable and experienced staff has been improving people's lives for more than 50 years. They collaborate closely with the government, community organizations, researchers, and their care partners—including families—to address needs, close gaps, resolve issues, and promote best practices. They are one of the institutions which is leading the way in senior care across Canada.
